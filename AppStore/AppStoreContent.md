## Description (4,000 characters max)
**Current: ~1,800 characters**

Master your Quran memorization journey with Quran Memorizer — the ultimate hifz companion designed to help you learn, practice, and retain the Holy Quran.

**A-B LOOP PLAYBACK**
The core feature every hafiz needs. Set custom start and end points to repeat any section of a surah. Perfect for memorizing verse by verse at your own pace. Adjust playback speed from 0.5x to 2x to match your learning style.

**100+ WORLD-CLASS RECITERS**
Choose from over 100 renowned Quran reciters including Mishary Rashid Alafasy, Saad Al-Ghamdi, Abdul Rahman Al-Sudais, and many more. Find the voice that resonates with your heart and helps you memorize effectively.

**OFFLINE DOWNLOADS**
Download any surah from any reciter directly to your device. Learn anywhere — on your commute, during travel, or in places without internet. Manage your downloads easily and free up space when needed.

**BEAUTIFUL READING EXPERIENCE**
Read along with authentic Uthmanic script Arabic text and English translations. Choose from 9 stunning reading themes including Sepia, Ocean, Midnight, Aurora, and more. Customize for comfortable reading day or night.

**TRACK YOUR PROGRESS**
Mark surahs as "In Progress" or "Memorized" to visualize your hifz journey. Track individual ayahs you've memorized with a simple double-tap. Watch your progress grow with beautiful visual indicators.

**FEATURES:**
• A-B loop playback for focused memorization
• 100+ reciters from QuranicAudio.com
• Offline downloads for any surah
• All 114 surahs with Arabic text
• English translations included
• 9 beautiful reading themes
• Playback speed control (0.5x - 2x)
• Surah and ayah-level progress tracking
• Dark and light mode support
• Fullscreen reading mode
• Search surahs by name or number

**PERFECT FOR:**
• Huffaz working on memorization
• Students learning new surahs
• Anyone wanting to improve Quran recitation
• Teachers helping students memorize
• Daily Quran reading and reflection

Start your hifz journey today with Quran Memorizer — beautifully designed, deeply focused on what matters most.
